📅 8-Week IELTS Study Plan

This roadmap helps you prepare step-by-step for the IELTS exam. Follow each week's focus and track your progress.

×

Modal Title

Week 1:
📘 Orientation & Reading Basics

Understand the test structure. Begin reading short academic texts daily.

  • ▶️ Explore IELTS overview: Official Format
  • 🧠 Learn scanning & skimming
  • 📖 Read 1 article daily (BBC, NatGeo)
Week 2:
📖 Reading Practice

Practice full reading sections with time management.

  • ⏱️ 60-minute full section practice
  • 📌 Learn question types: T/F/NG, MCQ
  • 📺 Watch: IELTS Liz Reading Tips
Week 3:
✍️ Writing Task 1

Understand bar, pie, and line charts. Write summaries with structure.

  • 📊 Learn visuals & vocabulary
  • ✍️ Write 3 Task 1 reports
  • 📎 Sample answers: IELTS-Up
Week 4:
🧠 Vocabulary Expansion

Focus on academic word lists and common IELTS topics.

  • 📚 Learn 50+ key academic words
  • 📝 Make flashcards & quiz yourself
  • 🎧 Listen & use new words in context
Week 5:
🗣️ Speaking Practice

Work on fluency, pronunciation and confidence.

  • 🎤 Practice Part 1, 2, 3 questions
  • 📼 Record and review your answers
  • 🤝 Practice with a partner or AI
Week 6:
✍️ Writing Task 2

Learn how to write opinion, discussion, and problem essays.

  • 📘 Understand essay structures
  • 🧠 Brainstorm + plan essays
  • 📝 Write and review 3 Task 2 essays
Week 7:
🎧 Listening Practice

Improve listening skills with real IELTS tests.

  • 🎧 Take 2 full listening tests
  • 📝 Review transcripts & answers
  • 📊 Analyze common mistakes
Week 8:
✅ Full Mock Test

Simulate the real test under timed conditions.

  • 📄 Take a full IELTS mock test
  • ⏳ Use exact test timing rules
  • 📈 Review results & set final goals

📖 Reading

3 passages, 40 questions. Focus on time and strategy.

  • Use skimming and scanning skills
  • Answer types: T/F/NG, Matching

✍️ Writing

2 tasks: Task 1 (150 words), Task 2 (250 words).

  • Graphs, maps, essays
  • Structure is key

🎧 Listening

4 recordings, 40 questions. Practice with audio daily.

  • Listen for key words
  • Check spelling carefully

🗣️ Speaking

3 parts with an examiner. Practice is essential!

  • Fluency > Accuracy
  • Record your answers
